Aqueous Ecology: Niagara (2024) comprised a series of film photographs of the Niagara region, soaked before development in water collected 100 yards upstream from Niagara Falls. The work was exhibited at Fitz Books in Buffalo, and published as a limited edition hardcover photo book. This project was generously supported by a $10k grant from the New York State Council on the Arts.
